Select Cans

Choose three graduated cans: 55 oz, 28 oz, and 15.5 oz so they nest smoothly.

Prepare Cans

Remove lids from the large & medium cans; cut both ends off the small can.

Trace Intake Hole

Use the small can lid as a template to mark a perfect circle on the medium can.

Cut Intake Hole

Puncture with a nail & hammer, then snip carefully along the outline.

Dry-Fit Chamber

Slide the small can through the hole, ensuring space for airflow.

Fuel-Feed & Air Slots

Cut two slots 25% down: one for air intake, one for feeding twigs.

Punch Exhaust Vents

Use a nail to punch vents around the top rim; crimp edges smooth.

Assemble & Test

Stack all cans, load fuel, and light to verify the rocket stove draft.
