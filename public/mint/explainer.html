Battle of the Raps: POAP & Knowledge Series

FaNa and the Art Life Group are proud to present the Battle of the Raps POAP initiative—a digital proof-of-attendance and recognition system designed to celebrate, certify, and archive the skills and contributions of Kenyan hip hop artists and fans.

Ethical Core

FaNa and Muppy stand on the principles of community building and development of a legitimate long term benefit of blockchain use rather than hype and wash trading focused market deveopment. Our efforts are dedicated to standing for people power- open source, decentralization, low energy use, and education to prevent scams.

What is a POAP?

A POAP (Proof of Attendance Protocol) is a collectible NFT issued to attendees of events, workshops, or key milestones. In this series, each POAP doubles as a digital credential—awarded to artists and fans who engage in curated learning modules and participate in battle events.

Why Mint on Solana?

We chose Solana for its speed, low transaction costs, and robust NFT infrastructure. POAPs minted here are publicly visible, transferable, and forever verifiable.

How It Works

  1. Connect your Solflare wallet on the Solana Testnet.
  2. Complete a hip hop learning module or participate in a battle event.
  3. Use the app to generate custom POAP art and mint it directly to your wallet.
  4. Explore all minted tokens via the POAP Explorer.

About the Series

This initiative is part of Fa Na’s broader mission to preserve and elevate East African hip hop heritage through digital culture. From Karen to Kibera, each module reflects our community's rhythm, resilience, and creative brilliance.
